Mission and vision

We build endocrine intelligence systems that help people and health systems act earlier, with dignity and trust.

MISSION

What we do, day to day

  • Capture meaningful signals with hybrid sensing that works on basic phones.
  • Turn data into interpretable insight using personal baselines and drift detection.
  • Connect insight to action through workflow-ready outputs for follow-up and prevention.
VISION

Where we are going

A world where endocrine transitions are not dismissed, and where early physiological drift becomes a trusted signal for prevention. We start with women because cyclical and life-stage endocrine transitions create clear longitudinal patterns and a major data gap, then expand to broader populations as evidence and partnerships grow.
